# **App Name**: ArPriceFinder

## Core Features:

- Product Search: Search for products (celulares, televisores, consolas, etc.) from various Argentinian online retailers.
- Price Comparison: Display prices from different retailers side-by-side for easy comparison.
- Filtering and Sorting: Filter products by price, retailer, and specifications. Sort results by price (low to high, high to low) and relevance.
- Product Details: Show detailed product specifications and descriptions aggregated from retailer websites. This may use a tool that uses reasoning to include information.
- Price History: Display the price history of a product to identify price trends.
- Alerts: User creates a price threshold for the app to monitor; send an email to the user when the item has dropped below that price.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) to suggest technology and currency savings.
- Background color: Dark blue (#00008B), a tint of the primary to let the search results pop.
- Accent color: Light cyan (#A0FFFF) for interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif with a modern, neutral, machined feel.
- Use clear, consistent icons for filtering, sorting, and product categories.
- Prioritize clear product presentation with high-quality images and concise information. Maintain a clean and organized layout for easy navigation.
- Subtle animations to enhance user experience, such as loading spinners and transition effects when filtering and sorting.
